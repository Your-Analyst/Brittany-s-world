I believe the test done in http://similarminds.com/ was valid in general and in my individual case, I believe most of the results was quite accurate. The Motiv Test gave me the type AxHWDxS (Ascetic, Haphazard, Withholding, Depressive and Sectarian). As for the Jung Test, I received the ISTP type which displayed being hidden, private, has trouble describing feelings, not very affectionate, loner tendencies, lower energy, can be insensitive to the misfortune of others etc. The personality disorder test varies with Paranoid, Schizoid, Antisocial and Avoidant being the highest of over 70%. These four disorders are mostly correct although for antisocial- I am 100% selfish or inconsiderate. I am more along the lines of caring for specific persons. The Enneagram test also varies with Type 4, Type 8 and Type 9 being the highest of 50 and over.
